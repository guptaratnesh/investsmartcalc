What We Do

InvestSmartCalc is a free online platform offering financial calculators designed specifically for the Indian market. Whether you are planning a SIP investment, calculating your EMI, understanding your income tax liability, or estimating your gratuity under the new Social Security Code 2020 — we have a calculator built for you.

All our calculators are updated for FY 2025-26 (Union Budget February 2025), apply the latest government rules including the Code of Wages 2019, Social Security Code 2020, and EPF Act, and are verified against official formulas.
