Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

✅ The short version We do not collect, store, or share your personal data. All calculations happen entirely in your browser. We don't use cookies for tracking and we don't serve ads.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information. InvestSmartCalc does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal details to use our calculators.

The financial figures you enter into our calculators (income, loan amount, investment amount, etc.) are processed entirely within your browser using JavaScript. These numbers are never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

2. Local Storage

We use your browser's localStorage for one purpose only: to remember your dark mode preference between visits. This is a small key-value entry stored on your device. It contains no personal information and is never transmitted anywhere.

You can clear this at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or site data.

3. Cookies

InvestSmartCalc does not use cookies for tracking, analytics, or advertising. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other third-party tracking scripts.

Your browser may create technical session cookies as part of normal web browsing. These are controlled by your browser, not by us, and contain no personal data related to this website.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts — To load the DM Sans and DM Mono typefaces. Google's servers receive your IP address when fonts are loaded. See Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not use any advertising networks, social media tracking pixels, or analytics platforms.

5. External Links

Our pages may contain links to external websites (government portals, official documentation). Once you leave our Website, this Privacy Policy no longer applies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

6. Children's Privacy

InvestSmartCalc is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page with the revised date. Continued use of the Website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised Policy.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach us through our Contact page.
